# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the spirit of the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v2.1): Respect, Inclusivity, Collaboration.

## Our Commitment

To foster an open and welcoming community environment, we commit to maintaining respectful and inclusive interactions in all spaces related to the project (Issues, PRs, discussions, etc.):

- Respect different perspectives, experiences, and backgrounds;
- Communicate and provide feedback in a constructive manner;
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks.

## Acceptable Behavior

- Friendly, professional, and respectful communication
- Driving discussions with facts and verifiable information
- Remaining restrained in disagreements and seeking consensus

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Insults, slander, personal attacks, harassment
- Sexual suggestions or discriminatory remarks and images
- Malicious spam, provocation, threats, or stalking

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not comply with this Code of Conduct, and may take restrictive measures against related accounts when necessary.
